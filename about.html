My Story

Even though I'm only sixteen and a half, I've already found a real passion in the world of development and coding. It feels like just yesterday I was tinkering with my first lines of code, and now I spend a significant amount of my time diving deep into various programming languages and projects.

What really sparked my interest was the power to create and build things from scratch using just lines of text. That initial curiosity quickly blossomed into a full-blown fascination. While I do a lot of general coding and development, I've found a particularly engaging niche in the FiveM community. There's something really exciting about contributing to and creating experiences within that platform. It's a fantastic way to see code come to life in a dynamic and interactive environment.

My core values revolve around continuous learning, problem-solving, and collaboration. The tech landscape is constantly evolving, and I believe staying curious and always seeking new knowledge is crucial. I also genuinely enjoy the challenge of tackling complex problems and finding elegant solutions through code. Finally, I value the opportunity to connect with other developers, share ideas, and learn from each other. It's an amazing community to be a part of, and I'm excited to see where my coding journey takes me next!
